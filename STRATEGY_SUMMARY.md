## ğŸ“Š How TikTok Uses Conversion Data

### Immediate Optimization

When users convert (click Follow), TikTok:

1. **Tracks the Event**: Records the conversion
2. **Analyzes the User**: What characteristics does this converter have?
3. **Finds Similar Users**: Uses machine learning to find more like them
4. **Shows Ads to Similar Users**: Prioritizes your ads to likely converters
5. **Improves Targeting**: Gets better at finding your ideal audience over time

1. **Export Emails**: Get list of converters

3. **Generate Lookalike**: TikTok finds users similar to your converters (1-3% similarity)
